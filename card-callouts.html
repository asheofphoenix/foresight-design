VR isn't just about Video Games

Virtual reality is being used in the healthcare field to treat depression, anxiety, autism, PTSD and even nicotine addiction with Mindcotine.

New Headway in Training and Education

With VR, it is possible to create simulated environments which can be used to train new employees. This has reduced a number of challenges and introduced a higher level of engagement and focus.
